Assistants are able to be connected to phone numbers for calls.

You can import from Twilio, Vonage, or create a free number directly from Vapi for use with your assistants.

  • Free Ahoi Number
  • Import Twillio
  • Import Vonage

Ahoi Phone Numbers are free of charge, and limited to 10 numbers per wallet.

Functionality to create Vapi phone numbers is only available for the US. For phone numbers from other regions, please use the Import Phone Numbers feature.
